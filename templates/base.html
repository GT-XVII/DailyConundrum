Welcome to Daily Conundrum

The rules of daily conundrum are simple: you get a scrambled word of 9 characters.
It is up to you to find the word hidden within the conundrum in 60 seconds, else the game is over.
Do you guess the word, then you will get another 60 seconds to guess the next one, and so forth.
The seconds that you have left every time you guess the correct word, will make up your final score.

Good luck & have fun!
